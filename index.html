Special Constables

Community Safety And Policing Act 2019

New Legislation

The Community Safety And Policing Act (CSPA) of 2019 has brought new changes to the way special constables are governed in the Province of Ontario.

Expectations

Minimum Requirements

  1. Position with approved Employer
  2. Canadian Citizen or Permanent Resident
  3. At least 18 years of age
  4. Physically and mentally fit for the role
  5. Completion of Ministry approved training
  6. Secondary School Diploma or higher education

Increased Accountability

  • Previously terminated officers cannot be appointed
  • Increased civilian oversight
  • Authorities specified on certificate of appointment
  • Increased investigation of alleged misconduct
